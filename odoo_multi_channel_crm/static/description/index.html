Odoo, a single solution for all your business related problems; it is widely known for its backend functionalities.

Customer Relationship Management is one of the most crucial parts of a business. Healthier relationship with your customer is directly related to enhanced profit of your business.

A CRM solution helps you focus on your organization's relationships with your customers throughout your lifecycle with them, including finding new customers, winning their business, and providing support and additional services throughout the relationship.

Presenting Odoo Multi-CRM Solution for you!

Odoo Multi-CRM Solution helps you to connect multiple customer relationship management solutions to your Odoo.

Feeds To Store Data in Simplified Manner

There are multiple relational fields in an Odoo form which makes it a little complex structure; especially while importing data from other platforms. Hence, to avoid the complication; Feeds are used in Odoo Multi-CRM Solution.

Importing/Exporting Data in Odoo

You can easily import company, contact, product, user, and deal/lead, from your CRM solution to Odoo.

Moreover, you can also Export company, contact, product, user and deal/lead from Odoo to your CRM solution.

You can also schedule Import functions via cron for company, contact, product, user, and deal/lead.

Refurbished Dynamic Dashboard

Introduced a dynamic dashboard to track the data of all the connected CRM to your Odoo.

Import the data of any CRM solution directly from the dashboard.

Well-designed Donut Chart and Line Graph to analyze the data.

The dashboard also offers Clickable tabs and data cards.
